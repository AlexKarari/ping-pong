PING PONG

  • About this game:

This is a web application that takes a number
from a user and returns a range of numbers from 1 to the chosen number with
a few exception. You get to play around with your choice of numbers to discover
which shall result to "Ping, Pong and PingPong!":

A user should be able to enter a new
number and see new results over and over again.

Game Time!

Let's play, won't we?

Enter a number, any number of your choosing. The results shall be displayed at the bottom of your number.

Rules.

The game shall be guided by the following set of rules:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping."
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong."
  3. Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "pingpong."

Let the fun begin!
